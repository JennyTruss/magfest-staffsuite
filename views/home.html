Location: Room 5211

This year's Staff Suite will be on the 5th floor of the hotel. We will have a balcony overlooking the atrium. Please be mindful of noise and keep the balcony clean should you choose to eat out there.

The Galaxy Cafe is the Gaylord's cafeteria for its own staff. The Gaylord is generously allowing MAGFest Staff access to the Galaxy Cafe during the listed hours. This service is at the staffer's own cost and can only be accessed during the times listed above.

To get to the Galaxy Cafe, go to the far back of the Maryland Ballroom foyer, then go through the doors and turn left. Just before you get to the Security desk in the hallway, look to your left and there will be a wide set of stairs going down. Go down those stairs and then the Galaxy Cafe will be on your right.

On Shift During a Meal?

Don't worry. You can still get food if you are unable to visit the Staff Suite because you are on shift. Just talk to your Department Head. They will take your order along with everyone else and send someone up to us to pick up food for everyone else.
